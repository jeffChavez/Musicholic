Mood and Tempo (Sonic Attributes)

Gracenote provides two metadata fields that describe the sonic attributes of an audio track. These fields, mood and tempo, are track-level descriptors that capture the unique characteristics of a specific recording.

MoodTrack-level perceptual descriptor of a piece of music, using emotional terminology that a typical listener might use to describe the audio track; includes hierarchical categories of increasing granularity. TempoTrack-level descriptor of the overall perceived speed or pace of the music; includes hierarchical categories of increasing granularity. Gracenote mood and tempo descriptor systems include hierarchical categories of increasing granularity, from very broad parent categories to more specific child categories.
